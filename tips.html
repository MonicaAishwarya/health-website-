Top Wellness Tips

1. Start Your Day with Water

Begin your morning with a glass of warm water. It boosts metabolism and hydrates your body.

2. Eat More Whole Foods

Choose fresh fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins over processed foods.

3. Move Every Day

Include at least 30 minutes of activity — walk, stretch, or dance daily.

4. Practice Mindful Eating

Eat slowly and avoid distractions to better understand your body's hunger signals.

5. Stay Hydrated

Aim for 6–8 glasses of water a day. Hydration helps with focus and energy.

6. Get Enough Sleep

Get 7–9 hours of sleep to let your body recover and strengthen immunity.

7. Reduce Sugar Intake

Limit sugary snacks and soft drinks. Excess sugar causes fatigue and mood swings.

8. Take Breaks from Screens

Stretch and rest your eyes every hour to improve posture and focus.

9. Practice Deep Breathing

Breathe deeply for 2 minutes daily. It reduces stress and calms the mind.

10. Keep a Positive Routine

Build habits like journaling, walking, or reading to stay consistent and positive.
